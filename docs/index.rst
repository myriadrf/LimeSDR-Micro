LimeSDR Micro is small form factor PCIe expansion card Software Defined Radio (SDR) board. 
Availabe in two different form factors:

* `LimeSDR Micro M.2 <M.2>`_ as M.2 expansion board
* `LimeSDR Micro <mPCIe>`_ as mini PCIe expansion board
